About SCS-RE

SCS-RE is a real estate research application designed to help users understand the Dubai residential property market.

  • Explore residential areas across Dubai
  • Review market and transaction information
  • Estimate potential property returns
  • Compare investment scenarios
  • Save selected areas and opportunities
  • Ask an AI assistant general real estate research questions
